Introduction to Responsive Web Design

Media Queries

Responsive Web Design (RWD) is an approach to web design that makes web pages render well on a variety of devices and window or screen sizes. Media queries are a key component of RWD, allowing developers to apply different styles based on the characteristics of the device, such as its width, height, orientation, and resolution.
